Lemon Chicken Parmesan

2 chicken breasts, raw
1 cup parmesan cheese, finely grated
1 fresh lemon, zested, juiced, and de-seeded
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flower

  1. Combine lemon juce, lemon zest, eggs, flower and parmesan in a bowl
  2. Whisk thoughly until mixed
  3. Dip chicken breasts in the mixture, coating thoughly
  4. Cook the chicken breasts over medium heat in a pan with olive oil for 6-8 minutes
  5. Serve with rice and enjoy!

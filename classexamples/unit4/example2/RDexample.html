More Information

Our agency puts a lot of care into finding the right homes for our dogs.

There is nothing worse thank placing a dog, only to find that they are unhappy or they come back to us because owners did not get what they expected.

Families seeking to adopt our dogs can expect to be thoroughly interviewed. We will also visit the dog in your home at random intervals during the first year. Further, you will need to visit the dog at our shelter 3 times before taking him or her home, and we will observe your interactions to ensure that you are a good fit for each other.
